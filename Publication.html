Experimental Publication

Brief: At the boundary between inside and outside, the most creative, powerful and surprising things can happen. There are so many interesting and quirky personal stories about spaces and people that are waiting to be told. What story would you like to communicate through text, image, and paper? For your final project, you need to ideate, design and produce a 24 – 36 page print publication with purely original content and visuals, inspired by the ‘inside-out’ theme. Aim The main aim of this task is for you to experiment with different image-making tools (camera, tablet, pen, ink, etc.) and different typographic treatments (scale, colour, width, shapes, tools etc.). Be creative in the way you capture, manipulate and process text and image with particular consideration for the way in which you unfold the ‘story’ to your intended audience. Make sure you opt for an innovative size, format, materials, and binding method that match the narrative you are conveying.
